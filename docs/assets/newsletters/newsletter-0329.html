What's next? Legislative action!

This week, we are emphasizing 4 bills, but since they are in the same committees, you can just send 2 messages to get them all!


The House Judiciary Committee is considering 2 bills passed by the Senate.

S.208 - No Secret Police - Law enforcement must show identification; no masks. Passed by the Senate and referred to the House Judiciary Committee who have heard testimony. The Senate version has very weak enforcement options ($1000 civil fine). Call on the House Judiciary Committee to toughen up the penalties.

S.209 - An act prohibiting civil arrest in sensitive locations. Passed by the Senate and referred to the House Judiciary Committee who have heard testimony. Ask the House Judiciary Committee to support this bill and send it to the floor for a vote.

The Senate Judiciary Committee is debating two bills, both passed by the House.

H.541 - Election interference. Passed by the House and sent to the Senate Judiciary Committee. No action yet. Ask the Senate Judiciary Committee to approve and send the bill to the full Senate.

H.849 - Civil suits for rights violations by law enforcement officers (including Federal agents). Passed by the House and referred to the Senate Judiciary Committee who have heard testimony. Reach out to the Senate Judiciary Committee to approve this bill so the full Senate can vote.
