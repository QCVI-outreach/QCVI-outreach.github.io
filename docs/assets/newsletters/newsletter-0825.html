**Vermont  Fair & ****Impartial **Policing Policy (FIPP)

The FIPP limits local police involvement in immigration enforcement: if an undocumented immigrant poses no threat to an officer or the public, officers should not share their personal information with Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) or Border Patrol (CBP). By law, all Vermont law enforcement agencies (sheriffs and municipal or state police) must adopt the policy and commit to treating everyone fairly. Community members are invited to provide comments on the latest version of the FIPP by September 1. You can find a copy of the policy here and an explainer here.
