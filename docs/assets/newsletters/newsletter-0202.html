General Membership Meeting - Bring the Rhythm, Bring the Family & be ready for our next NO KINGS march!

Tuesday, February 3  | 5:30-7:30 PM

Queen City Brewery | 703 Pine Street

Two special items on the agenda this Tueday: We'll make a racket with a build-a-rally-drum workshop. Join us for a family-friendly night of action! We'll have fun as a Citizen Drumming Group and get instruction from real-deal drummers. Bring your kids, some 3 gallon buckets (1 gal for smaller folks), and something to hit them with. We'll have the rest of the materials and tools. Plus, join us in sending Valentines to our state officials (sponsored by ACLU). We'll supply the materials for you to send notes of appreciation for helping to defend Democracy. We'll also have ICE OUT lawn signs and whistle brigade kits to help slow down kidnappings.
