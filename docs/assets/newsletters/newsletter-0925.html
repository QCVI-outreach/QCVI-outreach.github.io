No Kings!

No Kings Day is back!

Saturday, October 18 | 10:30 AM | Calahan Park, 2 Locust Street | Roosevelt Park, 57 Oak Street

We're hosting TWO big marches for No Kings Day #2. One will assemble at Callahan Park in the South End; the other will meet at Roosevelt Park in the Old North End. They will meet at City Hall Park at about noon. No speeches, just marching and singing with our neighbors. Brings all your friends, all your signs, and let's make some noise!


No Kings Day Poster Party

Tuesday, October 7 | 5:30 PM | Queen City Brewery, 703 Pine Street

This month's Indivisible Meet-up will feature:

  Poster making for the marches - get our message across. We'll bring supplies, you bring words!

  Orientation for Marshalls (no experience required) - help us march smoothly and safely. We'll show you how.

  Postcard writing - reach out to build awareness and community. We'll bring supplies, you bring your handwriting!

  New idea development. Get your own pro-Democracy action up and going. You bring ideas and we'll bring advice, support and resources!

Food and drink are available from Pizza 44 and Queen City Brewery.


Picket ICE with Justice for All

Friday, October 24 | 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM 

U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services , 75 Lower Welden St, Saint Albans, VT

Our friends from Indivisible Brattleboro have formed a group called Justice for All to picket the ICE office in St. Albans. No blockading or obstruction, just a peaceful moving sidewalk presence around the building to demand Human Rights for All, and an end to ICE agents being allowed to mask and travel in unmarked vehicles in Vermont.
