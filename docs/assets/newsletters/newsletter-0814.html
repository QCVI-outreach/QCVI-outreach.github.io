Meditation sit-in at ICE

Wednesdays | 4:30-5:30 PM

ICE Building | 188 Harvest Lane | Williston at Tafts Corners

We sit as silent witnesses to the activities being conducted inside the building. We don’t engage with the employees or people passing by, but we hope that our presence and our signs provoke some questions. Bring a bench, chair or cushion–whatever enables you to sit comfortably. If you bring a sign, we hope that the message will respectfully raise questions about ICE’s policies and actions, and convey our appreciation for migrants, and their right to fair and legal treatment. Our intention is not to judge the people who work in the building, but to change attitudes and policies. Join us!
