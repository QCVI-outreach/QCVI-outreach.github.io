Be a force for Justice AND Safety- Be a Marshal

We still need a few more volunteer Marshals for John Lewis Day. It's easy and fun and you get to wear a cool vest and keep folks safe in traffic. This event is a good beginners' intro to Marshaling as it will be smaller than No Kings Day with no marching on city streets. We provide training and a great benefit package: companionship, helping community, supporting a good cause, and the respect and envy of your neighbors. Contact: qcvimarshals@gmail.com


Donald Trump wants to make American Science his personal piggybank

Trump has proposed a new federal rule to create a committee of political appointees that would decide all Federal grants. No scientific evaluation, no ethical standards, no peer review. This would destroy America's scientific efforts, dismantle public health, decimate rural hospitals, and divert the budgets of the NIH, the CDC, the NSF, FEMA and dozens of other agencies to the pockets of Trump and his cronies. It is a naked grab for power and graft. What can you do? Make a comment on the proposed rule here by this Monday July 13. More details from the Union of Concerned Scientists here.
