Stop faceless thugs from roaming our streets

Our calls and emails about S209 (No warrantless arrests in protected places) saved the bill! It passed the House and will head back to the Senate. In the meantime, the House Judiciary Committee stripped federal agents from S208 which requires law enforcement to be identifiable and not cover their faces. Now is the time to reach out to your reps and tell them to put back the controls on federal agents. No secret police in Vermont!


Call the Sergeant-at-Arms (802-828-2228) and leave a message for your rep. They'll ask your name and town. Tell them to to add federal agents back into S208 (Law Enforcement Identification) and vote YES. Don't know your rep? Ask the Sergeant or look here.
