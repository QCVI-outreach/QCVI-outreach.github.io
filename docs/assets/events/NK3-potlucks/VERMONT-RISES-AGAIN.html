VERMONT RISES AGAIN. No Kings III Protest, March 28, 2026.

On March 28, Queen City Indivisible and the greater Burlington community will march with the rest of the nation to defend democracy, resist executive overreach and stand in solidarity with those terrorized by ICE. This peaceful protest will show the power of the people of Vermont, strong and united against injustice, at a time where community and collective resistance is more important than ever.

Two groups will march to City Hall Park. In the Old North End, protesters will gather at Roosevelt Park at 11:00 AM. In the South End, protesters will gather, same time, at Calahan Park. Both will begin marching at 11:30 AM, and will converge at City Hall Park at 12:30 PM. Additionally, in the New North End, 1127 North Ave., there will be a Honk & Wave for those who want to stand in solidarity. Want more info? Email qcvt2025@gmail.com.
