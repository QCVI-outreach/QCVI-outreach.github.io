Welcome to Queen City VT Indivisible.

We're a part of Indivisible.org - a grassroots movement of thousands of local groups with a mission to:
  • Elect progressive leaders,
  • Rebuild our democracy,
  • and Defeat the Trump agenda.

The QCVI Mission

Queen City Vermont Indivisible is a group of concerned individuals seeking to preserve democracy, resist autocracy, and empower our community through nonviolent protests, actions, and local events.

Why "Queen City"?

  • We formed in Burlington, Vermont - AKA the Queen City.
